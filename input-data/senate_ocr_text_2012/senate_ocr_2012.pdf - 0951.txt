Item 2 - Educational Background and Business Experience: Jose F. Gonzalez-Heres, CFA
is a managing director of Morgan Stanley Investment Management ("MSIM") and a portfolio manager for the Fund of Hedge Funds accounts at Morgan Stanley AIP. He is also a member of the Fund of Hedge Funds Investment Committee. He was promoted to portfolio manager in 2004 after having served as senior research analyst since 2001. Prior to joining MSIM, Mr. Gonzalez-Heres served as chief executive officer of Suggestionator, Inc., a privately held software company. He has also worked as an investment banker at Bear Stearns & Co., Inc. and Prudential Securities, Inc. Prior to his investment banking positions, Mr. Gonzalez-Heres worked at IBM Corporation in Engineering, Sales, Marketing and Consulting and at IBM Credit Corporation in its Corporate Finance Department. While at IBM, he earned a U.S. and international patent for his work on algorithms. Mr. Gonzalez-Heres has also published research in The Journal of Alternative Investments and The Journal of Fixed Income. He is a past board member of the City of Boca Raton Florida's Telecommunications Advisory Board. Mr. Gonzalez-Heres holds a B.S. in electrical engineering from Northwestern University and an M.B.A. in Finance and Investments from the Yale University School of Management. He was born in 1963.
