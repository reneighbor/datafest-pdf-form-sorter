The Select Committee on Ethics is required by section 106 of the Ethics in Government Act of 1978, as amended, to review public financial disclosure reports in order to determine whether they are in compliance with applicable laws and regulations.
Enclosed you will find a request for clarification or additional information, if necessary, related to your candidate disclosure report which we have reviewed as required by law. Please make the requested clarifications or provide the additional information, if necessary, on the enclosed page(s) from your public disclosure statement, sign and date each clarification or each item of additional information, and return to the Secretary of the Senate, Office of Public Records, Room 232, Hart Senate Office Building, United States Senate, Washington, D.C. 20510. Please return any Confidential Reports to the Select Committee on Ethics at Room 220 of the Hart Senate Office Building. The requested clarifications or additional information will be considered an Amendment to your Report and must be received by the Office of Public Records within thirty days of the above date.
If you believe that any clarification or additional information requested by the Committee is not necessary to finalize your report, separate from any amended pages, please provide the Committee with an explanation as to why the requested clarification or additional information is not necessary. If no clarification or additional information is necessary, please inform the Committee by letter, fax, or telephone within thirty days of the above date.
If you have any questions about this letter or your public financial disclosure requirements, please contact John Lewter with the Committee at (202) 224-2981.
Thank you for your attention regarding this matter.
