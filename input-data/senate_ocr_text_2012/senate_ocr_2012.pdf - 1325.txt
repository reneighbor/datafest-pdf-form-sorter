The following charts were prepared by the general partner to illustrate the monthly performance, and in the case of Charter WCM and Charter Aspect, the pro forma performance results, on a net asset value per unit basis, of each partnership versus that of the CASAM CISDM Public CPO Fund Index from inception of trading through February 29, 2008. The CASAM CISDM Public CPO Asset Weighted Index reflects the dollar-weighted performance of all public Commodity Pool Operators (CPOs) reporting to the CASAM CISDM Hedge FundlCTA Database. In order to be included in the CPO Public Asset Weighted Index universe, a fund or pool must have at least $500,000 under management and at least a 12-month track record.
