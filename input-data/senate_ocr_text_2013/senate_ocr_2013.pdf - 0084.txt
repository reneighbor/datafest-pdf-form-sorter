The Honorable Kay Bailey Hutchison Dallas, TX 7520y Dear Senator Hutchison:
This is in response to your request for an extension of time for filing your termination public financial disclosure report. Your form was originally due on February 1,2013. Pursuant to section 101(g) of the Ethics in Government Act of 1978, as amended, the Committee grants you a 90-day extension, and your form will therefore be due no later than May 2,2013. Section 101(g) also provides that the total of all extensions granted to an individual may not exceed ninety days.
Please note that section 104(d)(1) of the Act provides that any individual who files a report more than 30 days after the last day of the filing extension period shall pay a filing fee of $200.
